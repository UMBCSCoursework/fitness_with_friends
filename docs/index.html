Fitness With Friends

...

Building a virtual fitness User Interface
with a focus on enhancing user social experiences.

THE PROBLEM

...

Problem Statement

The COVID-19 pandemic spread rapidly across the world, quickly forcing governments to impose prolonged lockdowns and social distancing measures which, themselves, brought about a digital transformation that resulted in new forms of business – as well as social – engagements and interactions. Moreover, the pandemic has wreaked havoc in the United States, causing adverse experiences with enhanced levels of trauma as a result of continued social distancing or isolation.

With the continuation of the pandemic and the promulgation of both remote work and distanced learning, personal recovery may largely take place through the formation of safe and supportive environments that encourage sociability in an era of digital transformation.

OUR Approach

...

A Community Building Social Fitness App

We seek to engender the (much needed) positive psychological benefits that stem from social interactions, as well as physical activity, through our own “Fitness With Friends” app! The goal is to provide users an interface with multiple features: from scheduling live workouts with friends, to signing up for instructor lead workouts, and even to join virtual socials with other members of the community.

Expected Services For Our Users

...

Our plan is to develop an exciting User Interface
that caters to all persons seeking a virtual fitness experience,
either with friends or with certifed instructors!

USER GOALS

  1. Friendly Workouts

    2. Users may seek to view workout recordings, or attend instructor-led sessions, with friends. Groups of friends are able to view, and chat with, one another during these sessions."

  2. Accountabili-"buddies"

    3. Members seeking positive reinforcement through shared workout experiences can select, or even be paired with, other users with who to join recorded or live workout sessions with. The more a pair of accountabilibuddies join sessions together, the greater the in-app benefits or rewards that they are eligible for!

  3. Fun Competitions

    4. Users can search and sign up for small group competitions in either AMRAP ( as many as possible) or FF (fastest finsiher) categories, since the desire to ease up on one's self after a few rounds is greatly diminsihed in a group setting. The victor from any round is promoted to a new competition, in the style of a tournament bracket. "

USER ACTIONS

  1. Friendly Workouts

    2. Search our library of recorded workouts or sign up for a live session. Users are prompted with an option to invite other users by means of user ids. Invited users will thusly receive a notification with the details. Regarding live sessions, every user can toggle an interactive mode on, allowing instructors to encourage them with audio feedback. "

  2. Accountabili-"buddies"

    3. Users log both their biometric and goal-related information and can opt for activity monitoring across a few workouts. In this manner a user can be provided a list of potential accountabilibuddies - that boast similar goals and statistics - to choose from. Users are paired if both parties indicate an interest in working out together.

  3. Fun Competitions

    4. Users can select from our constantly-updating list of instructor-guided competitions. In order to compete a user must be present, and have their cameras on, for the duration of a competition. "

OBSTACLES

  • Though user support groups and the novelty of libraries that are constantly changing are known ways to enhance user motivation, creating an easy-to-use interface that seamlessly does so will be challenging
  • Integrating the use of other apps with ours - such as allowing users to listen to their favorite Spotify or iTunes playlist - may be a fun feature but may also be challenging to implement.
  • What sort of after workout details, training analysis charts or results be made visible on a user's account?
